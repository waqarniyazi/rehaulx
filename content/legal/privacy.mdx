This includes:

- **Profile Data**: User preferences, content creation history

- **Communication Data**: Messages, feedback, and support requests

## Content Data
When you use our service, we process:
- **Video Content**: YouTube URLs and video metadata you submit

- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information

- **Cookies and Tracking**: For functionality and analytics

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services**: Process your content through our AI systems

# 4. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the EU, you have these rights:

## Your Rights
- **Access**: Request copies of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate information
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke consent for processing

## Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on:
- **Consent**: For marketing communications and optional features
- **Contract**: To provide our services
- **Legitimate Interest**: For security, fraud prevention, service improvement
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with applicable laws

# 5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures:
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Limited access on need-to-know basis
